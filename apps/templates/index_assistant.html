Use Case Example

Helping a Solo Service Provider

Sarah runs a small beauty studio and manages all her client communication on WhatsApp. She often struggles to keep up with messages while providing treatments.

With Echo, Sarah’s WhatsApp becomes smarter: she can draft polite replies, get quick summaries of long chat threads, and even translate client requests into clear, professional responses.

This saves her hours each week, helps her stay responsive, and gives clients a more professional experience — all without leaving WhatsApp.
