Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Echo is a privacy-first AI assistant for professionals. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Who we are

Controller: ECHO LTD, ADDRESS: Echo Ltd, Sde Nehemia, Israel, Email: privacy@driftless.pro

Data we collect

  • Account details: name, WhatsApp number, and basic contact info provided by you.
  • Usage data: timestamps, feature usage, and technical logs to keep the service reliable.
  • Message content you explicitly send to Echo for AI assistance (e.g., to draft or summarize).

How we use data

  • Provide the service (AI assistance inside WhatsApp) and maintain reliability.
  • Prevent abuse, troubleshoot issues, and improve quality.
  • Communicate important service updates.

Retention

We keep data only as long as needed to deliver the service and meet legal obligations. On cancellation or deletion request, we remove associated personal data, subject to lawful retention requirements.

Processors & transfers

We use trusted providers to operate Echo (e.g., cloud hosting, AI model providers). These processors handle data on our behalf under contracts and appropriate safeguards. Current categories include: hosting/infrastructure, observability/logging, and AI inference providers.

Security

We apply technical and organizational measures to protect data (encryption in transit, access controls, least-privilege practices). No method is 100% secure, but we work to continuously improve.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to contact your local supervisory authority. To exercise rights, email privacy@driftless.pro.

Children

Echo is not directed to children. If you believe a child’s data was provided to us, contact us to remove it.

Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll post changes here and revise the “Last updated” date.
